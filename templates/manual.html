Smart Plug Control System

Welcome to your Smart Plug Dashboard. This manual will guide you through all features.

1. Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your main control center for your smart plug.

Device Status Indicators

  • ON — Device is powered on
  • OFF — Device is powered off

2. Understanding the Charts

The charts visualize energy consumption trends over time.

Chart Components

  • X-Axis — Measurement time
  • Y-Axis — Values (Watts, Volts, Amps)

3. Power Control

Turning On/Off

  1. Open the Control Section
  2. Click "Turn ON"
  3. Click "Turn OFF"

Safety Features

  • Overload protection
  • Live power monitoring
  • Remote control access

4. Energy Monitoring

  • Watts (W) — Power usage
  • Voltage (V) — Electrical potential
  • Current (A) — Charge flow

5. Troubleshooting

  • Device not responding — Check WiFi & power
  • Incorrect readings — Re-calibrate the device
  • Connection issues — Restart device & router
  • Download Manual — Use the PDF button above
